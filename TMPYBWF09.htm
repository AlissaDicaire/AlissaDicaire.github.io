Designer • Photographer • Classic Rock Lover • Happiest Outside

My name is Alissa Dicaire and I am a Graphic Designer in Ottawa, Canada. I am a recent graduate of the Graphic Design Program at Algonquin College. My main area's of focus are print, branding and layouts. I have experience working in the industry both freelance and corporate. I interned at Canadian Geographic Magazine for six weeks and worked as the graphic designer for Keep a Child Alive uOttawa Chapter. I am also currently freelancing and working on my growing portfolio. I am a passionate person and love colour, photography and going outside the lines! Check out my work below!
