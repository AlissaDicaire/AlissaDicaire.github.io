Who is Alissa?

I'm an old soul and seek the simple things in life. I love taking my car out for long drives, camping, traveling and skiing in the winter. I compulsively listen to Classic Rock and a map of my travels hangs over my bed. Most describe me as free spirited, adventerous and a humanitarian at heart. I am currently working on contract at Canadian Geographic as a graphic designer, working mostly on educational materials but not limited to layout design, production aid and branding.

Why Graphic Design?

It all started when I was a small child. My dad would show me his portfolio and I knew I wanted to be like him. Graphic Design allows me to express my other passions in a visually creative way. It allows me to explore new ways of doing something and to be specialized in an industry that has no limits. I am proficient in Adobe Illustrator, InDesign and Photoshop.

Print Design

Many say 'print is dead' but as long as I'm around, it wont be going anywhere. CMYK is my prefered setting, although I do enjoy working in RGB. I have gained much print experience working for Canadian Geographic Magazine, working in print production and preflighting the entire April 2016 issue.
