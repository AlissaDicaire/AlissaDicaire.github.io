My name is Alissa Dicaire and I am a Graphic Designer in Ottawa, Canada. I am a recent graduate of the Graphic Design Program at Algonquin College. I am very passionate about design and enjoy every aspect of the creative process, from the early stages to the finished product.

I have experience in graphic doing various work in a variety of different environments. I recently worked as a graphic design intern at Canadian Geographic Magazine, designing for multiple magazines, as well as doing work for the Royal Canadian Geographical Society and working on events such as the National Geographic Challenge. I have also done freelance work for a nubmer of companies and charities, some listed are the Canadian Journal of Law and Society and Keep a Child Alive, University of Ottawa Chapter. If you are looking for a well rounded graphic designer to do work for you, feel free to contact me any time!
