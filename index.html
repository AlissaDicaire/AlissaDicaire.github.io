Designer • Photographer • Classic Rock Lover • Easy Going • Happiest Outside

My name is Alissa Dicaire and I am a Graphic Design Student studying my final year at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada. My main focus and disciplines are illustration, photography and branding. I got into design for one main reason: to make a difference in the world. One colour at a time.

I design because I want people to stop and think and be impacted by my work. Or, just to simply help people. I don't want to fall into any stereotypes nor do I want anything I create to be ignored. If I can make a difference, or trigger a reaction, even in the slightest way, I know I am designing for the right reasons. I love exciting people and I feel that through art and design, I can capture people's imagination, thoughts and feelings. No matter the subject what it is I am creating, I want it to have meaning or a purpose. I want something dull and lifeless, to have colour and life. But I don't just want to make pretty things, I like to go deeper than that. I enjoy nature, simplicity and hardwork. I try to capture this in my creativity. I like going into the world, traveling (or just leaving the quiet work space of my bedroom), getting idea's and learning about new things. Not to mention experiencing life and being able to put that into a creative use. I believe that that makes good design and that's why I love Graphic Design.
