HELLO !

Who is Alissa?

I'm an old soul and seek the simple things in life. I love taking my car out for long drives, camping and skiing in the winter. I compulsively listen to The Eagles and Abbey Road hangs over my bed. I'm a small town girl who lives in the subarbs and I wouldn't have it any other way. Most describe me as free spirited, adventerous and a humanitarian at heart. Fun Fact: I am obsessed with geography and can list all 196 countries and tell you where they are on a map.

Why Graphic Design?

It all started when I was a small child. My dad would show me his portfolio and I was in love. I told myself when I grow up I will either become a biologist or an artist. I picked the latter because I am horrid in science. Graphic Design allows me express my other passions in a visually creative way. It allows me to explore new ways of doing something and to be specialized in an industry that has no limits. I am a newbie in this industry but I can't wait to discover my next project.

Print Design

Many say 'print is dead' but as long as I'm around, it wont be going anywhere. CMYK is my prefered setting, although I do enjoy working in RGB for certain things. I like seeing the physical object that I have created, even if it is just a magazine spread or a poster. Being able to hold a book or interact with a 3D model is what makes print my main displine - because it's not just on a screen. Print is a beautiful thing.

I am happiest outside, work hard and always learning.

Sleep, eat, go camping, design, repeat.
